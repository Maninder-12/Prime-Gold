FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do I need an appointment?

No—walk-ins are welcome. If you’d like a quieter time, contact us and we’ll schedule it.

How do you determine the offer?

We consider purity, weight, condition, and current market demand. We’ll explain the breakdown clearly.

What if I don’t accept the offer?

No problem. There’s no obligation—your items remain yours.

Do you buy broken or mismatched jewelry?

Yes. Broken chains, single earrings, scrap gold, and old pieces are commonly purchased.
