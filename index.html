FAQ

Quick answers to common questions buyers ask.

Do I need an appointment?

No—walk-ins are welcome. If you’d like a quieter time or a house call, contact us and we’ll schedule it.

How do you determine the offer?

We consider karat/purity, weight, condition, and current market demand. We’ll explain the breakdown clearly.

What if I don’t accept the offer?

No problem. There’s no obligation—your items remain yours.

Do you buy broken or mismatched jewelry?

Yes. Broken chains, single earrings, scrap gold, and old pieces are commonly purchased.

Can’t make it to the store?

Ask about a free house call (availability varies by location and items).
